About Rapid-Speed Rafting

Our Mission

Our services are geared towards experienced and novice thrill-seekers alike, who expect quality products that never fail in dangerous situations. Rapid Speed Rafting offers an exhilirating experience to customers, while also ensuring quality and safety. Through our products and services, we guarantee that customers will be satisfied and thrilled.

Our Legacy

The best Whitewater Rafting in Colorado, White Water Rafting Company offers rafting on the Colorado and Roaring Fork Rivers in Glenwood Springs. Since 1974, we have been family owned and operated, rafting the Shoshone section of Glenwood Canyon and beyond.

Our Promise

Trips vary from mild and great for families, to trips exclusively for physically fit and experienced rafters. No matter what type of river adventures you are seeking, White Water Rafting Company can make it happen for you.
